Guildlines

1. Read up on how we will be conducting the campaign in your society.

2. Fill up the Form 1 and download the permission brochure from our website. We’ll also be sending the same to you via mail.

3. The permission brochure will contain all the necessary details. All you’ll need to do is just show the same to the respective authority in your society, and request permission to conduct the campaign. (Such an authority could be your society Secretary/President or any such committee member.)

4. Once he/she approves, fill up the Form 2 to give us your bank details. This will enable us to reward you for your efforts in helping organise the campaign. Also, request the respective committee member for his details (name, contact no, and address details) so that we may contact him.

5. Once all this is taken care of, we contact the committee member to decide on a date to begin the campaign. We will also be explaining to him/her the other important details and processes.

The Campaign Story

1

The campaign will span a total of 3-5 days, or as long as the society deems necessary.

Our special customised donation stands will be put up in all the prominent places of your society. This is done to ensure maximum outreach and participation during the campaign. (We insist on placing these stands in the lobbies of every one of your society’s buildings. Other stations for these stands will be decided with the help of the committee member.)

Posters will be put up on notice boards and other appropriate places during the campaign to ensure residents know about the campaign. We will also make use of social networking platforms such as WhatsApp and Facebook to achieve the same.

2

The customised donation stands

Each stand will be 1-1.5m high, and will occupy very little space. (Can be easily setup anywhere!)

Each stand will have a form holder from where people can pick up a form to see the details of the NGO/Orphanage (official details, their objective, work, current achievement, how the money raised would be used and much more).

A donation box where they can donate the money.

A display screen (or space) which will give residents and other participants all the details of the campaign and other instructions on how to donate.

3    

After donation, they will need to fill a form online (instructions on how to do so will be included in the form itself) for the sake of transparency. On completion of this form, participants will receive an online receipt stating their participation in the campaign.


Once the campaign is closed officially, all the collected articles and funds will be transported to the respective NGOs/orphanages.

Rewards

The initiative you took towards helping host the campaign will undoubtedly touch many lives, lives which haven’t seen as many privileges as us. We will make sure everyone in your society comes to know about your initiative-every one of the campaign’s posters will have your name as the host of the campaign.

A cash reward of Rs 5000 and certificates for successfully having successfully hosted the campaign.

The campaign will consist of 2 fundraisers which will be conducted within a span of six months. The first will be fundraiser to raise money, and the second, a drive to collect old household articles like clothes, books, etc.

On successful completion of the first campaign, you shall receive Rs 3000 along with a certificate. 

On successful completion of the second campaign, you shall receive Rs 2000 along with a certificate.
